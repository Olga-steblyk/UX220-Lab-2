Hello, today I will talk about the most recent book I read!

Over the summer, i took hobby of reading. It is something I am trying to evolve as a skill. My latest read is the book called 'Motherthing', as it was recommended by the nice lady at a book store. Written by Ainslie Hogarth, the horror novel mentions the themes of loneliness and abandonment left by a parental figure. While this books goes in to disturbing graphics about the detail and actions in the book, I found the book very fascinating.

The plot of the story includes topics such; murder, cannibalization, depression, suicide,and much more. The plot follows Abby and Ralph, after the suicide of Ralph's mother, Lauren, Ralph inability to accept the loss of his mother while Abby has to deal with the passing of her untrusting and 'unlovebale' mother in law. The story follows Abby and her haunting by her former mother-in-law and her method of coping with her mental termoil.
